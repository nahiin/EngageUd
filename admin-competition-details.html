RWADTHON Competition

PMU Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship

About the Competition
Brief Description

RWADTHON is a premier innovation challenge aimed at bringing together the brightest minds to develop cutting-edge technological solutions for sustainable development.

Details

Comprehensive details regarding the stages, tracks, and requirements. The competition spans three days where participants will ideate, prototype, and pitch to a panel of expert judges from the industry.

Objectives
  • Foster innovation among students and professionals.
  • Develop solutions for real-world problems.
  • Encourage multidisciplinary collaboration and networking.
Evaluation Criteria
  • Innovation & Originality (30%)
  • Feasibility (30%)
  • Market Impact (20%)
  • Presentation (20%)
Rules & Guidelines
  • Must be a PMU student.
  • Teams of 2 to 5 members.
